About Brew & Bite

Located in the heart of Houston, Brew & Bite has been serving the community with passion and dedication since 2020. We believe in the perfect blend of quality coffee and delicious food, creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere for everyone who walks through our doors.

Our skilled baristas are trained to craft the perfect cup of coffee, using only the finest beans sourced from local roasters. Each cup is prepared with care and attention to detail, ensuring every sip delivers the rich, bold flavors our customers love.

Our kitchen team takes pride in preparing fresh sandwiches daily, using locally sourced ingredients whenever possible. From our signature breakfast sandwiches to our hearty lunch options, every item on our menu is made with love and attention to quality.

At Brew & Bite, we're more than just a cafe - we're a community gathering place where friends meet, ideas are born, and memories are made. Whether you're stopping by for your morning coffee or settling in for an afternoon of work, we're here to make your experience exceptional.
